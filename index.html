hi,

my name's Michael. I'm an undergraduate student at the College of William & Mary in Virginia, USA, where I spend most of my time pursuing my interest in mathematics and physics. My hopeful environmental vision and fascination with turbulent phenomena together manifest as a broad interest in theoretical and computational plasma physics. My current research interests include:

  • turbulence and transport in magnetically confined plasmas
  • hidden symmetries and stellarator optimization
  • device simulation and modeling
  • magnetic reconnection (in both fusion and space plasmas)

Visit my projects page for information about the sort of things I've worked on. Thank you for reading my website :)
