A visual introduction to magnetic coordinate systems

In my path to an intuitive understanding of the various magnetic coordinate systems which exist to study magnetic confinement concepts, I thought it might be helpful to produce a visual guide. This exposition follows the one found in [1], and assumes some familiarity with vector calculus. $E=mc^2$.
